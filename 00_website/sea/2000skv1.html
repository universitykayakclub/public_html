2000 Sea Kayak Trip Schedule

Return to UKC Index

Below is a list of all the club sea kayaking trips scheduled in 2000.  There were 33 trips scheduled in total.  Three trips were run as private trips due to an insufficient number of club paddlers (3) signing up.  One trip was cancelled altogether due to an insufficient number of paddlers.  Two trips were cancelled due to inclement weather.  The trip schedule varies year by year depending on the preferences of the coordinators.  As far as recent years go, 2000 was a fairly typical year of club activity.  This list should give you a good idea of how frequently sea kayak trips run, when they run, where they run, and the distribution of difficulty level.
