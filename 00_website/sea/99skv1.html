1999 Sea Kayak Trip Schedule

Return to UKC Index

Below is a list of all the club sea kayaking trips scheduled in 1999.  There were 30 trips scheduled in total.  Two of these trips did not run because the minimum number of paddlers to use club gear (3) did not sign up.  The trip schedule varies year by year depending on the preferences of the coordinators.  As far as recent years go, 1999 was a fairly typical year of club activity.  This list should give you a good idea of how frequently sea kayak trips run, when they run, where they run, and the distribution of difficulty level.
