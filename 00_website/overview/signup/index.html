UKC Signup: Step by Step

Fill out the requirement forms:

  • You have two options here
    • You can print them yourself
    • You can get and fill them out at pool or lake session
  • Regardless of which option you choose, please go to the What to do next section

UKC Information Sheet

  1. Print the sheet, which is found here.
  2. This sheet has most of the information regarding the University Kayak Club and is for you to keep.

Individual Responsibility Waiver

  1. Print the waiver, which is found here.
  2. Print your full name at the top. You can leave the Card # field blank. It refers to your UKC membership number, which will be written on the back of your membership card when you successfull complete all the steps for joining.
  3. Read both the first and second page.
  4. Once you read and understand this waiver, please sign and date it at the bottom of the second page.

Membership Data Form

  1. Print the form, which is found here.

  2. The fields circled in yellow below are mandatory, but you may fill in the others also. Note, spouses should leave the Student/Staff Number field blank.


  3. Check which membership option you want. See the Membership Overview for definitions of these membership levels.


  4. Check how you are affiliated with the University of Washington. The WAC fee is black out because it is likely to change, however it is usually less than $10.00 a quarter. Make sure to check the Summer quarter box so you get your membership at half the price.


  5. Now go to the second page of the form.

  6. Although the title of this section says Interests, really this means what email lists do you want to be subscribed to? The items circled in yellow are the only current lists, except for ukc-all@u... which every member is subscribed to. Check which ever lists you want to be subscribed to. For a description of these lists, go to the Listserver page.


  7. The rest of this form consists of statements in which you must initial and date after have completing. Please pay attention to the disclaimer in bold, shown below. The Statement of Responsibility is the next form, the Safety Guidelines can be found here, and you will demonstrate a wet exit at a pool or lake session.

What to do next

  1. You need to attend a pool or lake session. Visit the UKC homepage frequently for announcements regarding these events.
    1. During Fall, Winter, and Spring quarters, pool sessions are held. To attend, meet at the Waterfront Activity Center at 8:30 AM on selected Sundays.
    2. During Summer quarter, lake sessions are held. To attend, meet at the Waterfront Activity Center at the time and date specified in the announcement.
  2. If you have printed the forms above and filled them out, please remember to bring them or you will have to fill them out again.
  3. Which ever signup session you attend, pool or lake, you will have to fill out the IMA Waiver Card.
  4. Next all the new members will do their swim test, which consists of the following actions.
    1. Enter the kayak
    2. Flip the kayak over
    3. Pull the skirt off of the cockpit rim of the kayak
    4. Slip out of the kayak while underwater
    5. After resurfacing, swim with all of your gear approximately 25 yrds out and 25 yrds back
  5. After returning to the UKC room at the WAC, new members will pay their fee
  6. After everyone has paid and the paper work is complete, a mandatory tour and logistics talk is given
  7. Finally, new members will recieve their membership card
